lasagna dish

Description

Lasagna is a delicious layered Italian dish made with pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and herbs

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook noodles according to package directions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan. Add marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Simmer.
  3. Mix ricotta, egg, parsley, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Layer: Spread sauce, then noodles, ricotta mix, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover with foil and bake for 25 mins. Uncover and bake 10 more mins. Let it rest before serving. Enjoy!
