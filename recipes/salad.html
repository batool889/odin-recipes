Salad

Description:

Salad is a fresh, healthy dish made with raw vegetables, often served with a light dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups lettuce or mixed greens
  • 1 tomato, chopped 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Place in a bowl and toss gently.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice or vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Mix and serve fresh.
