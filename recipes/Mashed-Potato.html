Mashed Potatoes

Description:

Mashed Potatoes are a creamy, comforting side dish made by mashing boiled potatoes with butter and milk.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (about 4–5) potatoes (Yukon Gold or Russet)
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup milk (or cream)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into chunks.
  2. Boil in salted water until tender (about 15–20 mins).
  3. Drain and mash with butter and warm milk.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Mix until smooth and creamy. Serve warm!
